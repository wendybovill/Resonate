Tools To Manage Anxiety

Resonate is a website that is dedicated to providing useful resources and information to those who battle with Anxiety. This may be ongoing anxiety or reactive anxiety. Please provide feedback to assist us to help you in future.

Resonate also provides an App which has free CBT tools, Mindfulness Resources, and daily positive content to give you your "Daily Boost".

On our Contact Page is a form, which serves as research for us to be able to develop our products further, to suit your needs. Please provide us with feedback, this is so valuable to us!

Relaxing Activities


To help ease anxiety you could try the following:

  • Walking
  • Drinking Tea/Beverage in the Park/Outdoors
  • Doing a mild Workout
  • Going for a run in nature/along the seaside
  • Walking your dog/friends dog
  • Going fishing
  • Taking up a hobby like photography
  • Reading a relaxing book in fresh air
  • Doing Self Care
  • Eating properly
  • Practice Relaxation Techniques
  • Taking vitamin supplements especially B vitamins
  • Talking to friends or a therapist
  • Running
  • Baking
  • Doodling
  • Drawing
  • Journaling
  • Making Goals
  • Practicing Gratitude for 5 things daily
  • People Watching
  • Cloud Watching
  • Staying Hydrated
  • Practicing Meditation
  • Taking care of your health
  • Not being afraid to say 'No'
